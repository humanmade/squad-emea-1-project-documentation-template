# Definition of Ready

The _Definition of Ready_ describes what a user story must cover before the team can start working on it. 

* User Story is formulated and implementable within one sprint
* Acceptance Criteria are formulated and complete
* Common understanding of story achieved during Sprint Refinement/Planning Meeting
* All necessary information or other resources are available
* Dependencies are clear and there are no blockers to this story
* Sub-tasks are defined (where applicable)
* User Story is prioritised (and again within the sprint)
* All Stories & Tasks have estimates
